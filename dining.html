Delight in Every Bite

Nestled in the heart of historic Winchester, The Wessex Restaurant at Winchester Wessex Hotel by Sunday offers unforgettable dining experiences. Enjoy fine dining or bespoke private dining events, with seasonal menus showcasing the best of British cuisine.

Our Restaurant

The Wessex Restaurant

The Wessex Restaurant, our signature fine dining venue, offers an elegant setting with seasonal menus featuring locally sourced ingredients, perfect for special occasions. Located on the ground floor, it accommodates up to 100 guests in a refined ambiance.

The Lounge and Cathedral View Terrace are also available for dining.
