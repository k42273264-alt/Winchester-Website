Getting Here

Arrive Easily

Nearest station: Winchester – 0.7 miles

Local buses connect to Winchester city centre, stopping near Paternoster Row.

From M3:

Exit at Junction 9 (Winchester).

Follow A31 towards Winchester city centre.

Turn onto Paternoster Row; the hotel is next to the Cathedral.

20 miles to Southampton Airport.

Pet-Friendly

Welcome Four-Legged Guests

Our dog-friendly rooms come with treats, bowls, and access to Winchester’s historic city and countryside walks. Dogs must be on leads in public areas and supervised (max two dogs under 15kg, £20/night per pet, service dogs free).
