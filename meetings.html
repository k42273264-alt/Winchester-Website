Meetings & Events

Versatile Event Spaces

Host your corporate meetings, training sessions, or private gatherings at Winchester Wessex Hotel by Sunday. Our flexible event spaces, including the grand Cathedral Suite and Amber Brasserie, offer modern amenities and historic charm, all steps from Winchester Cathedral. All rooms are conveniently located on the ground floor.

Our Venues

Flexible Event Spaces

Cathedral Suite

265.98 m² – Combines Wykeham Suite and Amber Brasserie for large dinners and functions, accommodating up to 300 for receptions, 150 for banquets, or 130 for dinner/dance events.

Wykeham Suite

94.86 m² – Ideal for corporate meetings or banquets, with a capacity of up to 100 for receptions or 80 in theatre style.

Austen Suite

33.75 m² – Perfect for small meetings or boardroom setups, accommodating up to 26 in theatre style.

St. Swithun Suite

23 m² – Intimate space for boardroom meetings, suitable for up to 10 guests.

St Giles 1 & 2

54 m² – Flexible combined space for meetings or receptions, hosting up to 40 guests.
