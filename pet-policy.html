Pet Policy

Bring Your Pet to Winchester

Welcome to Winchester Wessex Hotel by Sunday

We are excited to extend a warm welcome to you and your four-legged travellers. Located near Winchester Cathedral and surrounded by historic city walks and countryside trails, our hotel is the perfect place for you and your dogs. Please review the helpful information for dog owners below.

Allowed Dogs

  • Dogs are allowed in our Classic and Superior Rooms.
  • A maximum of two dogs per room; must weigh less than 15kg unless hotel management gives prior approval.
  • A £20 per night fee applies. If two small dogs stay together, only one charge applies.
  • Service dogs are free of charge.
  • Dogs are welcome in the bar but not in our restaurant (with the exception of service dogs). They must be on leads at all times.
  • Dogs cannot be left alone in rooms. Housekeeping will only clean rooms when dogs are not present.

Guest Responsibilities, Damages, and Cleaning

  • Dogs must be supervised and on leads at all times.
  • Owners understand that dogs should not disrupt the quiet enjoyment of other guests due to excessive barking.
  • Owners must clean up after dogs and dispose of waste properly (no dog waste bins in the garden).
  • Guests are responsible for any damage caused by dogs.
  • A £150 cleaning fee will apply for any extra cleaning or damage after check-out.

Restrictions

  • Only dogs are allowed.
  • Assistance dogs are exempt from charges and restrictions.

Amenities

  • Treats | Water bowl | Toy ball
  • Disposal bags | Bin
  • Information on nearby countryside walks, including South Downs National Park trails

By bringing a dog, guests agree to these terms. Thank you for helping us maintain a comfortable environment for everyone.
